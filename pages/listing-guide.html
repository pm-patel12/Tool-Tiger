Listing Guide for Software, Tools & Apps

Welcome to ToolTiger, your trusted platform dedicated exclusively to software, digital tools, and applications. To maintain the highest standards of quality and relevance, please carefully follow our guidelines when submitting your product for listing.

  1. Eligible Listings
    • ToolTiger accepts only software products, digital tools, and applications across all industries and platforms (web, desktop, mobile).
    • Products must be original, legally licensed, and safe for users. We do not allow pirated, cracked, or unauthorized software.
    • Each product should offer clear value and functionality to its users.
  2. Submission Requirements

    3. To ensure your listing is informative and useful to our community, please provide the following information:

    • Product Name: Use the official name of your software or tool.
    • Description: Provide a clear, detailed, and accurate summary of your product’s features and benefits (minimum 150 characters). Avoid vague or misleading descriptions.
    • Visual Assets: Upload a high-quality product logo, screenshot, or promotional image that accurately represents your software or app.
    • Official Website or Purchase Link: Include a direct and working URL where users can learn more, download, or purchase your product.
    • Categories: Select all relevant categories and tags that best describe your product (e.g., Productivity, Marketing, AI, Design, Finance, etc.) to help users find it easily.
    • Pricing Details: Clearly state your pricing model—whether the product is free, freemium, subscription-based, or a one-time purchase.
  3. Content Standards and Quality
    • All listing content must be truthful, clear, and professional. Misleading claims or exaggerated features are prohibited.
    • Language should be polished and free of grammatical errors.
    • Avoid overly promotional or spammy language; keep the focus on factual information.
    • Ensure that any claims made (e.g., “fastest tool,” “best in class”) can be reasonably supported.
    • The content should be appropriate for a broad audience and free from offensive or discriminatory language.
  4. Review and Approval Process
    • Once submitted, your listing will enter our moderation queue for manual review to ensure compliance with our guidelines.
    • Free plan listings may take up to 7 days to be reviewed and published.
    • Listings under Basic, Pro, or Sponsored plans are prioritized and typically reviewed within 24 to 48 hours.
    • We reserve the right to reject or remove any listing that does not meet our standards or violates our policies, without prior notice.
  5. Listing Plans and Features

    6. Free Listing

    Ideal for: Beginners who want to get listed

    Price: $0 (Free forever)

    Features:

    • Basic listing visibility
    • Free forever
    • Limited to one category and a few tags
    • Limited search exposure
    • Listing review and approval may take 7 or more days

    Limitations:

    • No verified green badge
    • No custom call-to-action (CTA) button styling
    • Cannot add your own social media links

    Pro Listing

    Ideal for: Full listings with complete product details

    Price: $5 (currently available for $2)

    Features:

    • Full listing with complete details
    • Lifetime access
    • Multiple categories and tags
    • Improved search visibility
    • Listing published within 24 hours
    • Verified green badge for credibility
    • Ability to style your CTA button
    • Option to add your own social media links
  6. Upgrading and Editing Your Listing
    • You can upgrade your listing plan anytime to unlock additional features and faster review.
    • Editing your listing is easy through your user dashboard, but significant content changes may trigger a new review process to maintain quality.
    • Keep your listing information up-to-date to ensure the best experience for users.
  7. Prohibited Content

    8. To protect our users and maintain quality, the following content is strictly prohibited:

    • Listings for pirated, cracked, or unauthorized software.
    • Links or software containing malware, viruses, or harmful code.
    • Misleading, false, or unverifiable product claims.
    • Spammy or repetitive listings.
    • Offensive, discriminatory, or inappropriate content.
    • Any content violating intellectual property rights or copyrights.

    • Listing on ToolTiger does not imply endorsement or affiliation with our platform.
